Police vs. Crime

Visualizing crime and police in the United States

This site provides an overview and comparisons of varous different government datasets, allowing us to see the correlation between police actions and the amount of crime in the area. The data we have is divided by state, allowing us to look at how differences across states can affect the results of the data. Hopefully, these reports can help police departments, officers, and elected officials make better decisions to keep citizens safe.
